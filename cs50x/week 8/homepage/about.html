About Me

I am a 25-year-old backend developer based in Baku, Azerbaijan. I work at PASHA Insurance on the Common Business Solutions team, building systems with C#, .NET Core, Entity Framework, and SQL Server.

My background is in Chemical Engineering, but I found my passion in programming. I studied at 42 Warsaw coding school in Poland and previously worked at EPAM with Java. Since then I have been building my skills in backend development and databases.

I have completed multiple Harvard CS50 courses including CS50x, CS50 SQL, CS50 Python, CS50 Business, and CS50 Scratch. Learning how things work from first principles is what drives me.
